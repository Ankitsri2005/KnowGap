📖 Our Story

Our Mission: Closing the Gap

knowGap was founded on a simple realization: Most tests tell you what you got wrong, but they don't tell you why or how to fix it.

The Forensic Approach

We don't just grade papers. Our AI forensically maps every incorrect response to a foundational concept. If you miss a Calculus question, we check if the gap is actually in Algebra. By identifying the root cause, we save students hundreds of hours of inefficient studying.

Our AI engine analyzes patterns across multiple dimensions — topic mastery, difficulty levels, and conceptual dependencies — to build a complete picture of each student's learning landscape.
